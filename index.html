Original Dataset Quality

5.73M total flight records spanning 12 months with significant data quality challenges including missing values in critical delay columns and temporal distribution imbalances requiring strategic intervention.

Missing Value Strategy

Implemented sophisticated imputation: 0-fill for delays (indicating on-time performance), row removal for critical identifiers, and median imputation for distance to maintain analytical integrity.

Temporal Rebalancing

Addressed January overrepresentation through stratified sampling, ensuring equal monthly representation while preserving within-month variance and operational patterns.

Feature Engineering

Created 24 derived features including temporal indicators (IS_WEEKEND, IS_PEAK_HOUR), delay classifications, and operational metrics to enhance analytical depth.

Detailed Data Cleaning Pipeline

Step 1: Data Quality Assessment

  • Initial Dataset: 5.73M flight records from 2015
  • Missing Values Identified: 86,153 missing departure delays, 105,071 missing arrival delays
  • Data Quality Issues: January overrepresentation (temporal imbalance)
  • Critical Columns: Verified airline codes, airport codes, flight dates

Step 2: Missing Value Treatment

  • Delay Columns: Filled with 0 (indicating on-time performance)
  • Distance Column: Median imputation to preserve route characteristics
  • Critical Identifiers: Removed rows with missing airline/airport codes
  • Validation: Post-imputation data quality checks performed

Step 3: Stratified Sampling

  • Issue: January had disproportionate representation
  • Solution: Equal monthly sampling (100K total sample)
  • Method: Stratified random sampling preserving within-month patterns
  • Result: Balanced temporal representation for unbiased analysis

Step 4: Feature Engineering

  • Temporal Features: IS_WEEKEND, IS_PEAK_HOUR, TIME_BLOCK_2H
  • Delay Classifications: Binary flags for different delay thresholds
  • Operational Metrics: Cost calculations, efficiency indicators
  • Total Features: 24 engineered variables for enhanced analysis
